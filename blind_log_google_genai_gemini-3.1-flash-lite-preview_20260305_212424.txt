### 2. The Structure (12 Words)
A 12-word seed phrase represents 128 bits of entropy (randomness). The structure is as follows:
*   **Entropy:** The first 11 words represent the raw random data.

### 3. Ordering Matters
The order of the words is critical. Because the words are converted into a binary seed, changing the order of even two words results in a completely different private key.
